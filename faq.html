When is SugarCon 2016?

What is SugarCon?

SugarCon is SugarCRM's annual conference for customers, partners, developers and technology experts to explore the latest CRM trends and innovations. The conference includes three days packed with keynotes, breakout sessions and networking opportunities, including the SugarCon Conference Party.

Who should attend?

If you’re looking to improve business performance by building extraordinary relationships with your customers, you should attend SugarCon 2016. The conference will be a dramatic first step toward business transformation.

Of course, if you’re responsible for the use, management, or adoption of the SugarCRM Platform in your organization, SugarCon just can’t be missed. It’s the place to learn about the latest product enhancements and best practices for using CRM to transform your business.

Need to brush up on your skills? Register to attend SugarCRM training sessions led by SugarCRM University's expert trainers.

REGISTRATION

How much does it cost to attend SugarCon 2016?

Early Bird registration: $795
Standard registration: $995
Buy 4 passes / Get 1 pass free: Purchase four (4) full conference passes at the standard registration rate, get one (1) full complimentary conference pass at no charge.

What is included with my registration fee?

Your SugarCon 2016 registration fee includes access to the conference, conference materials, access and food at the Welcome reception on June 13 and SugarCon Party on June 14. Admission to the three days of the conference, featuring presentations by SugarCRM customers, industry analysts and SugarCRM executives, access to the partner pavilion and endless networking opportunities. Breakfast and lunch on both days, plus food during the evening networking functions.

Could I purchase a conference pass at the event?

Onsite registration was not available. We wanted to make every effort to be prepared for you when you arrived at SugarCon 2016, therefore we asked that you registered before May 20, 2016.

What was the registration substitution and cancellation policy?

Cancellation Policy:

If you cancelled your registration for any reason, you must have notified us in writing before February 20, 2016 for a refund of your registration fees paid, less a $100 processing fee. A request for cancellation must have been provided in writing by email to sugarcon2016@sugarcrm.com and the cancellation was effective upon receipt by SugarCRM, provided that such written notice of cancellation was received by February 20, 2016. Cancellations within 60 days of the first day of the Event are non-refundable, and paid attendees who did not attend or who canceled after the deadline of February 20, 2016 were liable for the entire fee.


Transfer Policy:

You may have transfered your registration to another person without incurring additional fees, if the requested transfer was 30 or more days from the first day of the Event, by providing a written request which included proper authorization from the transferor to transfer its paid registration to an identified transferee, to sugarcon2016@sugarcrm.com. However, if the requested transfer was received within 30 days of the first day of the Event, such request would have required a payment of a $50 transfer fee.


Other Terms & Conditions:

In the unlikely event of cancellation of the Event, the sole and maximum liability of SugarCRM is limited to a full refund of paid registration fees received by SugarCRM.

TRAINING

What was included in Training?

Hands-on product training with Sugar University experts. Training consisted of two half-day courses on Thursday, April 23.

Topics includes:

  • Creating Reports
  • Working in Studio
  • Emailing in Sugar
  • Managing Users, Teams, and Roles

Did I need to bring my computer for training courses?

Yes, you should have brought your computer, everything else was provided.

Was there a separate fee for the SugarCRM Training on Thursday, April 23?

Yes, all training and related materials were separate from the conference registration fee. Training was only open to those who had registered for the SugarCon 2016 Conference. Space was limited and allocated on a first registered first served basis. Note: Space was limited to 25 attendees per class.

What is the expected weather in San Francisco in April?

San Francisco weather can be unpredictable and can change from block to block! The month of April is characterized by daily highs around 58°F and low temperatures around 46°F. We recommend packing with layers: shirts, sweaters, pants, and a jacket to allow for varying temperatures.
